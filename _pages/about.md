Hi! I am Haoxiang Wang (王浩翔), an undergraduate research intern at the [Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology](https://www.icst.pku.edu.cn/), Peking University.

Research Interests
======
My research interests include:
- **Differential Privacy** — privacy-preserving data generation and analysis
- **LLM Alignment** — aligning large language models with human preferences
- **Reinforcement Learning** — decision-making and policy optimization
- **Multimodal LLM** — large language models with vision and other modalities
