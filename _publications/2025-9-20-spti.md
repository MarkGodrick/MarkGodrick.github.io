We propose **SPTI** (Private Textual Intermediaries), a training-free framework for generating high-resolution differentially private (DP) synthetic images. Instead of operating directly in the image domain, SPTI converts private images into textual descriptions, applies DP text generation via a modified Private Evolution algorithm, and reconstructs images using text-to-image models — all with off-the-shelf models and no fine-tuning. SPTI significantly outperforms prior DP image synthesis methods, achieving FID = 26.71 on LSUN Bedroom and FID = 33.27 on MM-CelebA-HQ at ε=1.0.
